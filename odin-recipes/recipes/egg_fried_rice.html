Egg Fried Rice

Description

Egg fried rice is a quick, savoury Chinese stir-fry dish made by scrambling eggs and rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Peanut oil
  • 5 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 shallots, diced
  • 3 eggs
  • 3 cups of leftover rice
  • 1 tbsp white pepper
  • 1 tbsp MSG
  • 3 sticks spring onion, diced
  • 3 cloves worth of ginger
  • 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp seasame oil
