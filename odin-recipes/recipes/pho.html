Beef Pho

Description

Beef Pho is a traditional Vietnamese noodle soup.

It consist of a rich, aromatic broth, flat rice noodles, herbs, and beef.

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions
  • 150g ginge
  • 10 star anise
  • 4 cinnamon quills
  • 3 cloves
  • 1.5 tbsp coriander seeds
  • 1.5kg beef brisketp
  • 1kg meaty beef bones
  • 1kg marrow bones
  • 3.5 litres quarts water
  • 50g dried rice sticks
  • 30g beef tenderloin, raw, very thinly sliced
  • Handful of Beansports
  • Thail basil
  • Cilantro, 3-5 springs
  • Lime wedges
