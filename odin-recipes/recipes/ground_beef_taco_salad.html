Ground Beef Taco Salad

Description

A healthy taco salad made of ground beef, mexican spices, and veggies.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounce container of romaine lettuce
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil divided
  • 2 ears corn
  • 1 lb lean ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning
  • 1 can low-sodium kidney beans
  • 2 avocado
  • Salsa
  • Salt
  • Pepper
